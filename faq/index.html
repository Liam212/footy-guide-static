FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Why can't I find a match?

Start by broadening your filters (sport, country, competition, broadcaster). If a match still isn't listed, it may not be available in the current dataset yet.

What timezone are match times shown in?

Times are displayed as provided by the data source. If a time looks wrong, compare with your local broadcaster listing.

Can I share a filtered view?

Use the evergreen landing pages (like football) for stable sharing. The date selector uses a URL parameter for convenience.

Where do broadcaster names come from?

Broadcaster listings come from third-party sources and may not match your local naming exactly.
