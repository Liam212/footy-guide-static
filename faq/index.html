Watching Live Sports

How can I find where to watch a match live?

Where Is Match helps you discover where to watch live sport on TV or streaming services by showing broadcasters, competitions, and match schedules in one place. Use filters to find matches by sport, country, or competition and instantly see available viewing options.

What channel is the match on today?

Match listings show the broadcasters and TV channels provided by third-party data sources. Select your sport or competition to see live sports on TV today and upcoming broadcast schedules.

Can I see live football on TV today?

Yes. Use the football filter or visit the football landing page to view live football matches, kickoff times, and broadcasters showing games today and upcoming fixtures.

Does Where Is Match show sports streaming services?

Where Is Match lists broadcasters and streaming platforms when available in the dataset. Availability may vary by country depending on broadcast rights and regional coverage.

Can I check sports on TV tonight?

Yes. Browse matches by date to see sports on TV tonight, including football, rugby, cricket, tennis, and other live events scheduled across broadcasters.

Why are some matches not available in my country?

Broadcast rights differ between regions. A match may be available in one country but not another depending on licensing agreements and broadcaster coverage.

Are match times shown in my local timezone?

Match times are displayed based on the provided data source. Always confirm with your local broadcaster if exact kickoff timing is critical.

Is Where Is Match a streaming service?

No. Where Is Match is a live sports TV guide and match finder that helps you discover where matches are being broadcast or streamed legally.
